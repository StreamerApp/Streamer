# How to Get API Keys from Universal Resolvers


## Orionoid
- Visit the [Orionoid website](https://orionoid.com) and create an account or log in with your existing account.
- Go to the [API page](https://panel.orionoid.com/) and click on the **Generate** button under the **API Key** section. You will see your API key displayed on the screen. You can also copy it to your clipboard by clicking on the **Copy** button.

## Premiumize
- Visit the [Premiumize website](https://www.premiumize.me/) and create an account or log in with your existing account.
- Go to the [Account page](https://www.premiumize.me/account) and scroll down to the **API Key** section. You will see your API key displayed on the screen. You can also copy it to your clipboard by clicking on the **Copy** button.

## AllDebrid
- Visit the [AllDebrid website](https://alldebrid.com/) and create an account or log in with your existing account.
- Go to the [API page](https://alldebrid.com/apikeys/) and click on the **Generate** button under the **API Key** section. You will see your API key displayed on the screen. You can also copy it to your clipboard by clicking on the **Copy** button.

## DebridLink
- Visit the [DebridLink website](https://debrid-link.com/) and create an account or log in with your existing account.
- Go to the [Account page](https://debrid-link.com/webapp/account). in the **Passkey** section click on "show" button. The API key will appear.

## Offcloud
- Visit the [Offcloud website](https://offcloud.com/) and create an account or log in with your existing account.
- Go to the [Settings page](https://offcloud.com/#/account) and click on the **API Key** tab. You will see your API key displayed on the screen. You can also copy it to your clipboard by clicking on the **Copy** button.

## Real-Debrid
- Visit the [Real-Debrid website](https://real-debrid.com/) and create an account or log in with your existing account.
- Go to the [API Key page](https://real-debrid.com/apitoken). You will see your API key displayed on the screen.
